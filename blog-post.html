O'Shea's Fisheries new website coming soon.

Starting out as a new web developer was not easy for me. I can still remember the first time I saw my first bit of code, I was shocked and everything looked too complicated to me. I was contemplating whether I should study it further. I can now say that it was the best decision for me to join Friends of design and study interactive media and web design. I have learnt so much over this past year in the interactive media course from how movies are made with Premeire Pro and After Effects, to sound and foley on Reaper Audio.

In the web design course i learnt how right basic code starting from Html and then working my way into css, javascript, Jquery, Vue JS, and Php. With all this new web design knowledge I decided to create custom website for my parents Lillian & Rubin O'Shea. They have done so much for me in my life that I have decided to return the favour and create a new company for them called "O'Shea's Fisheries". My parents have been running a fish business since 2012 and it has been taking off pretty well because of the amount of clientele they have.

We now have a mobile food trailer and will be selling fish from different events and locations. The website will be used as our marketing tool to display our Menus, promotions, deliveries and contact details.
